# Concept

**Project:** lone-ant
**Date:** 2026-07-11
**Status:** seedling

## In plain language

You play as a single ant, not the whole colony. The colony around you is a
living, breathing thing — other ants come and go, food gets stored, the nest
changes — but *you* are one ant with a body in the world. You walk around,
pick up food, carry it home, dodge the things that want to eat you, and the
colony slowly grows because of what you and the others do.

It is inspired by the old game SimAnt, but instead of commanding a whole
colony from above, you live down on the ground as one of them. The world
keeps going between play sessions, so when you come back, time has passed and
the colony is a little different than you left it. It runs entirely on your
phone, no internet, no accounts.

The big idea: you are small, the world is big, and your little actions add up
to a colony that survives or doesn't.

## The one-liner

Live as one ant in a colony that keeps living, growing, and remembering you,
even when you're not playing.
